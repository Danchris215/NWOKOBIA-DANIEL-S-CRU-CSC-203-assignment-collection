Conspiracy Theories in Football

The 2024 Ballon d'Or took place at Paris, France. It is one of the most anticipated and rewarding experiences for any football player. There were favorites to win this prestigious award amongst whom were Vinicius Junior, Rodri from Spain and Jude Bellingham.

News came out few hours before the winners had emerged that no Real madrid player attended the event. Even the head coach and owner of Real Madrid FC did not attend the event. This was because they felt UEFA was being unfair to them. They felt Vinicius deserved the award and even if it would have been given to a player from Spain based on their conquest at the Euros, Carvajal should have been given the award.

It has been filled with conspiracy theories of unjust treatment. Comments from prominent figures also indicates this. Such comments include:

Honestly, this award lost its credibility in 2018 when Cristiano did not win it- Zinedine Zidane The Ballon d'or and The Best are losing credibility-Cristiano Ronaldo
If the criteria of the award do not proclaim Vinicius the winner, those same criteria must proclaim Carvajal the winner.Since this has not been the case, it is obvious that the Ballon d'or and UEFA do not respect Real Madrid. And Real Madrid is not where it is not respected.- Unnamed Real Madrid source.
